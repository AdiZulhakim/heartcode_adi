Pet Violence

Cruelty to animals, also called animal abuse, animal neglect or simply animal cruelty, is the infliction by omission or by commission by humans of suffering or harm upon any non-human animal, regardless of whether the act is against the law.


Abandonment and Neglect

Pets are abandoned for a variety of reasons. Often, when abandoned, pets are forced to fend for themselves, and become feral. In particular, cats and dogs go to colonies. Feral cats are said to outnumber feral dogs. Feral cats are difficult to deal with, as often they become impossible to socialize enough to be re-introduced to a new human owner. In general, only some newly abandoned cats and very young feral kittens can be tamed.
